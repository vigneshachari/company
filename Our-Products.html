Our Products

In our bid to fight morbidity and mortality due to cervical cancer, we have developed an advanced AI powered Cervical Cancer Detection System to facilitate early diagnosis. This system is a suite of 3 products which work cohesively to obtain quick and accurate test results

Intellistain

Aindra IS is an automated stainer for staining biological samples that are mounted on glass slides. Aindra IS uses state of the art mechatronics and software to achieve unparalled staining. clear and high quality staining without eating up a hug space in the laboratory. Aindra IS is a one-stop solution for all staining requirements

VisionX

Tele-pathology: Aindra VisionX gives extremely Crisp images of a whole slide . These image enable extremely fast turn around times for the report by enabling images captured at the point of care to be transferred to a Clinician, thus enabling telepatholgy.

Digital images also cease prevent that is caused to clinicians as it allows them to analyse these images in the comfort of either their homes or at their workplace.

Astra

Aindra Astra is an AI platform developed to facilitate computational pathology for faster and more accurate diagnosis.

Astra is built from data that is assessed by experienced senior pathologists.

ClustrPath

CLUSTR is the only digital platform that is equipped with the entire case workflow along with high definition WSI viewing capabilities. These images can be accessed by pathologists anytime from anywhere. The stored images can be archived later for review, research, education, and presentation purposes.
