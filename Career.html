Looking for Senior Software Developer

Are you a passionate Techie ninja with 5 - 7 yrs of experience, who thinks, “I don’t want to just keep pushing paper or doing dumb work and want to create products that change people’s lives”. Someone who has the passion to create and not just the mentality to maintain existing systems. An Entrepreneurial spirit with a startup mentality, who will take pride in what he/she will help create. A believer and not a skeptic with an ability to wear multiple hats required in astartup.

Company Profile:

At AIndra Systems, an exciting early stage Start-Up based out of Bangalore, our Vision is to build world class systems to aid diagnosis of fatal diseases like cancer with the use of Artificial Intelligence. Our Mission is to use Computer Vision as the vehicle. We take immense pride in the fact that we are amongst the very few deep technology startups in India. We are working on using deep-tech like Optics, Mechatronics and AI in Healthcare. We have been featured by Forbes, Economic Times, Your story etc. We have been supported by multiple national as well as international agencies. We have multiple patent pending products which are under National Phase applications stage in multiple countries.

As a Senior Software Developer, you will be responsible for leading the implementation of the product architecture. We want you if you can:

1. A person with a great aptitude matched with an amazing attitude and work ethic
2. Someone who is unfazed by the speed of development and implementation.
3. Someone who loves new challenges and working on new requirements.
4. Capable of implementing scalable and high-performance systems.
5. Want to be involved in product development from an early stage of product conceptualization.
6. Capable of implementing solutions to be hosted on the Cloud.
7. Cloud computing knowledge and experience with deploying applications on any of the well known IAAS/PAAS providers.
8. Capable of providing reliable solutions to a variety of problems using sound problem-solving techniques.
9. Capable of performing technical root cause analysis and outlining corrective action for problems using debuggers, log files etc.
10.A quick learner willing to work on new programming languages depending on requirement.
11.The ability to communicate technical information clearly and succinctly to both technical and non-technical teams

Highly Desirable:

1. BS/BTech/MS/MTech (or equivalent degrees) in Computer Science or related technical disciplines

Required Technical and Professional Expertise :

1. Strong expertise in Python, Java, JEE, JSP, Servlets, JDBC, XML, Spring Framework, Hibernate, MVC, SQL, REST APIs, JAX-RS, Web Services & design patterns.
2. Hands-on experience in Mysql or Postgres with excellent understanding of SQL queries, stored procedure, DB Design etc
3. Hands-on experience in Tomcat and JBoss.
4. Hands-on experience in Linux/Ubuntu platform.
5. Should have experience in using tools such as Eclipse/IntelliJ/NetBean, ANT/Maven, Jenkin/ Cruisecontrol, JUnit etc
6. Hands-on experience of using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
7. Strong knowledge or working experience with REST APIs, frameworks and components
8. Experience of working in any of the JavaScript frameworks Backbone.js/ Angular.js/ ember.js/ node.js is a big plus.
9. Knowledge/Experience of any NoSQL database (MongoDB, CouchDB, Cassandra etc)

Job Perks

1. Performance based Stock options
2. Access to top academic research profs from a few IITs
3. Beer punch with the team at every second Friday's of the month
4. Company sponsored passes to technical & entrepreneurship events and meet ups

Please send your Details & your Resume to contactus@aindra.in
